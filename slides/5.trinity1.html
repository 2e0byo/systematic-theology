Councils and Popes

Nicea I (325)

We believe in One God, the Father Almighty, maker of all things visible and invisible. And in one Lord Jesus Christ the Son of God, the only-begotten born of the Father, who is of the substance of the Father, God from God, Light from Light, True God from True God, Begotten natum, not Made, consubstantial with the father, through whom all things were made in heaven and on earth, who for our salvation came down, was incarnate and made Man, suffered and rose the third day and ascended into heaven, he will come to judge the living and the dead. And [we believe] in the Holy Ghost.

But those who say "He was; and at one time he was not", and "before he was born, he was not" and "who was made from nothing" or from another substance or essence, or that the Son of God is changeable or mutable---these the Catholic Church anathematises.

— DZ125
